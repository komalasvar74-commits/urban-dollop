Raigad, a district in Maharashtra, India, is renowned for its historical significance and natural beauty. It's home to the majestic Raigad Fort, once the capital of the Maratha Empire, and a testament to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's vision of Hindavi Swarajya. The district also boasts pristine beaches, lush landscapes, and diverse flora and fauna, particularly in the Western Ghats. Raigad's rich cultural past is reflected in religious sites like the Ashtavinayak Temples and the Elephanta Caves, and its vibrant multicultural population.
